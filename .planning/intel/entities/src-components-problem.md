# Problem.jsx

## Purpose

Marketing section that articulates the problems solved by the Driveshaft Cable product. Presents four key hazards of unsecured driveshafts during towing (unexpected drop, transmission damage, costly failures, safety hazards) with a prominent warning box listing potential consequences. Creates urgency by highlighting risks and costs.
